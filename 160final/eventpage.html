Free Speech at Berkeley

Nov 13, 2017| Ex-Chancellor Dirks| Barrows 404

Ex-Chancellor Dirks will be discussing the nature of free speech in universities, particularly as it applies in Berkeley. Freed from the trappings of office and the self-censorship that comes with it, Dirks will provide a refreshingly un-filtered take on how the university has handled the most recent free speech events. Given the unique political climate that Berkeley espouses, the talk should prove to be scintillating for all involved.
